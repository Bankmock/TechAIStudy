Passport Photo Requirements & Guidelines

To ensure your photos meet official standards:

Photo Composition

  • Face must cover 70-80% of photo area
  • Eyes should be at 1/3 to 1/2 of photo height
  • Neutral facial expression (no smiling)
  • Both edges of face visible

Appearance

  • Plain white or light-colored background
  • No shadows on face or background
  • No glasses (unless medically required)
  • No headwear (religious exceptions allowed)

Technical Specifications

  • High resolution (600 dpi recommended)
  • Proper brightness and contrast
  • No red-eye or flash reflection
  • Recent photo (taken within last 6 months)
