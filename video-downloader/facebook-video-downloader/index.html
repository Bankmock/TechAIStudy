Is It Legal to Download Facebook Videos?

The legality of downloading Facebook videos depends on several factors:

  • Copyright: Most content on Facebook is protected by copyright. Downloading copyrighted material without permission may violate copyright laws.
  • Facebook Terms of Service: Facebook prohibits downloading content through third-party tools in their terms of service.
  • Fair Use: In some cases, downloading for personal, non-commercial use may fall under "fair use" provisions.
  • Public Content: Content from public accounts is more likely to be considered fair for personal use than private account content.

Our Recommendation: Only download videos that you have created, that are in the public domain, or for which you have obtained permission from the copyright holder. Our tool is provided for educational purposes and we do not encourage copyright infringement.
