Is It Legal to Download YouTube Videos?

The legality of downloading YouTube videos depends on several factors:

  • Copyright: Most content on YouTube is protected by copyright. Downloading copyrighted material without permission may violate copyright laws.
  • YouTube Terms of Service: YouTube prohibits downloading content through third-party tools in their terms of service.
  • Fair Use: In some cases, downloading for personal, non-commercial use may fall under "fair use" provisions.
  • Public Domain/Creative Commons: Content marked as public domain or with Creative Commons licenses can typically be downloaded legally.

Our Recommendation: Only download videos that you have created, that are in the public domain, or for which you have obtained permission from the copyright holder. Our tool is provided for educational purposes and we do not encourage copyright infringement.
