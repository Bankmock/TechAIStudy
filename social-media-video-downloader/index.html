Is It Legal to Download Videos?

The legality of downloading videos from social media depends on several factors:

  • Copyright: Most content on social media is protected by copyright. Downloading copyrighted material without permission may violate copyright laws.
  • Terms of Service: Many platforms prohibit downloading content through third-party tools in their terms of service.
  • Fair Use: In some cases, downloading for personal, non-commercial use may fall under "fair use" provisions.
  • Public Domain: Content marked as public domain or with Creative Commons licenses can typically be downloaded legally.

Our Recommendation: Only download videos that you have created, that are in the public domain, or for which you have obtained permission from the copyright holder. Our tool is provided for educational purposes and we do not encourage copyright infringement.
