What is Image to PDF Conversion?

Image to PDF conversion is the process of transforming image files, such as JPEG, PNG, or GIF, into a Portable Document Format (PDF) file. This allows you to combine multiple images into a single document, preserve image quality, and make sharing or printing easier. PDFs are universally supported and provide a more structured way to store or distribute visual content.
