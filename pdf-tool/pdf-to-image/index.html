What is a PDF to Image Converter?

A PDF to Image Converter is a specialized tool that transforms PDF documents into image files (JPG, PNG, etc.), either by converting each page to an image or extracting embedded pictures from the PDF. Unlike basic PDF tools, our converter offers advanced features like selective page conversion, quality adjustment, and batch processing. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is there any limit to the PDF size I can convert?

You can convert PDFs up to 50 pages at once, with each file under 20MB. For larger documents, we recommend splitting them first.

Will the converted images maintain my PDF's quality?

Yes, by default we preserve the original quality. You can increase or decrease DPI settings as needed.

Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

For security reasons, our tool cannot process encrypted or password-protected PDF files.

How secure is my PDF during conversion?

Extremely secure - all processing happens in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to our servers.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to images?

Yes, our tool works perfectly with both digital and scanned PDF documents.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes, our converter is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

Why would my converted images be blurry?

This usually indicates low DPI in the original PDF. Try increasing the output DPI setting.
