Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is there a limit to how many PDFs I can merge?

You can merge up to 20 PDF files at once, with each file under 50MB. For larger projects, we recommend merging in batches.

Will the merged PDF maintain bookmarks and links?

Yes, our advanced merger preserves most document elements including bookmarks, links, and form fields.

Can I merge password-protected PDFs?

For security reasons, our tool cannot process encrypted or password-protected PDF files.

How secure is my data during merging?

Extremely secure - all processing happens in your browser. Your PDFs are never uploaded to our servers.

Can I merge different page size PDFs?

Yes, our tool handles mixed page sizes. You can choose to normalize sizes or preserve originals.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes, our merger is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

Why would my merged PDF be larger than the originals?

This is normal as metadata from all files is combined. Use our optimization option to reduce size.
