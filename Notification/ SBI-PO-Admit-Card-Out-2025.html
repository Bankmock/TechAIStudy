SBI PO Admit Card 2025 Out – Download Now

State Bank of India (SBI) has officially released the Probationary Officer (PO) Admit Card 2025. Candidates who have applied can now download their hall tickets from the official website.

Important Details:

  • Exam Name: SBI PO 2025
  • Admit Card Release Date: February 2025
  • Exam Date: March 2025
  • Official Website: sbi.co.in

Steps to Download SBI PO Admit Card 2025:

  1. Visit the official website: sbi.co.in
  2. Click on the "Careers" section.
  3. Find the "SBI PO Admit Card 2025" link.
  4. Enter your registration number and password.
  5. Click "Submit" to view your admit card.
  6. Download and take a printout for future reference.
