Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What if the discount is "Buy One Get One Free"?

Our calculator is designed for percentage-based discounts. For "Buy One Get One Free" offers, you would typically calculate the effective discount yourself (e.g., if two items cost ₹100, and you get one free, you pay ₹100 for two, making it ₹50 per item or a 50% effective discount if you compare to buying two at full price).

Does this calculator handle sales tax after the discount?

No, this specific calculator only calculates the discount. To include sales tax, you would first use this tool to find the final price after the discount, and then use a separate sales tax calculator (or our percentage calculator) to add the sales tax to that final price.

Can I calculate what percentage discount was applied if I know the original and final price?

Yes, you can use our **Percentage Change Calculator** (or "X is what percent of Y" feature in our general percentage calculator). The formula would be: $((\text{Original Price} - \text{Final Price}) / \text{Original Price}) \times 100\%$

Is my price information secure with this online tool?

Yes, your privacy is important. All calculations are performed locally in your web browser. None of the price or discount information you enter is sent to our servers or stored in any way.

What are common discount percentages I might encounter?

Common discount percentages include 10%, 20%, 25%, 30%, 40%, 50%, and sometimes even higher during major sales events like Diwali, Black Friday, or End-of-Season sales.
