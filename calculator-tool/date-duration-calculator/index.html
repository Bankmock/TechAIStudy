Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does the calculator count the start date or end date?

When calculating the duration between two dates, our calculator typically counts the number of full days *between* the two selected dates. If you need to include both the start and end date, you might add 1 to the total days result, depending on your specific requirement.

Can I calculate business days only?

Our current calculator calculates total days, including weekends. For a business day (workday) calculation, you would need a specialized workday calculator that excludes Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays.

How does it handle partial months or years?

When calculating duration, it provides results in full years, months, and remaining days. For example, 1 year, 2 months, 5 days. For 'Add/Subtract' functionality, it correctly increments/decrements the date considering month lengths and leap years.

Is my date information saved or tracked?

No, your input dates are not saved or tracked by our servers. All calculations are performed instantly and locally in your web browser, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is a leap year, and why is it important for date calculations?

A leap year occurs every four years (with some exceptions) and has 366 days instead of 365, with February having 29 days. It's crucial for accurate date calculations because failing to account for it can lead to off-by-one-day errors over long periods.
