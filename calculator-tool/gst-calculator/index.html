Understanding GST Calculation Methods

Our calculator supports all GST calculation approaches:

Standard GST Calculation

Calculates tax amount from base price:
Base Price × (GST%/100) = GST Amount

Reverse GST Calculation

Extracts base price from total:
Total Amount ÷ (1 + GST%/100) = Base Price

Composition Scheme

For small businesses:
Turnover × Composition Rate = Tax Liability

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What's the difference between CGST, SGST and IGST?

CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) apply to intra-state transactions, while IGST (Integrated GST) applies to inter-state transactions. Our calculator automatically determines the correct tax type based on your needs.

How accurate is this GST calculator?

Our calculator provides 100% accurate results based on current Indian GST laws and is regularly updated with any tax rate changes.
